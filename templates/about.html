Our Vision & Mission

At PatchEZ, our vision is to bridge the gap between AI and Healthcare, by integrating machine learning models with advanced Iontophoresis-based wearable technology, enabling real-time stress detection and personalized drug delivery to enhance patient safety and mental well-being.

Goal

We aim to revolutionize the Indian healthcare system by building an affordable and scalable solution that monitors patients' mental and physical health 24/7, and provides instant, accurate drug release based on real-time stress levels, thereby reducing dependency on heavy medications like antidepressants and painkillers.

Why PatchEZ?

  • Real-time Stress Level Detection with ML Model
  • Smart Drug Release using Iontophoresis Technology
  • Cloud-Based Patient Monitoring with IoT
  • Secured Data Handling
